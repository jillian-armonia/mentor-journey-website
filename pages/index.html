About Me

I'm Jillian and I never expected myself to become a teacher, but I love guiding and helping people. I think a mentor fits me better because I have been mentoring many students, may it be about the English language, or keeping and sharing your cultural identity. I don't like the idea of following a curriculum, hence my refusal to call myself a full-fledged teacher, but I love to promote learning. I hope this site will be helpful to you if you're like me who wants to keep others motivated in learning!

My Lesson Plans

The lesson plans I have posted on this website are not all my own. I take a lot of inspiration from my own work experience and from the internet. However, I always try to take my own creative spin on it or I try my best to adjust it to my mentees. I firmly believe in meeting students where they are at, so I hope these will be useful for you to play with!
